Pack like a pro
Leave no trace

Image : Lizzie Streit, healthyforlifemeals.com, CC BY-SA 2.0

You're going to need lots of food and drink to sustain you for the duration of your trip. So you won't go hungry, it's a good idea to plan, and even prepare some of your meals before you go. This not only helps to simplify the camping cooking experience, but shopping for ingredients is easier too. Salt and pepper is recommended to bring for seasoning food.
